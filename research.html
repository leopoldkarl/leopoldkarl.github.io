Research

I'm at the very start of my Research Career, so there's not too much to find here yet. However, two topics I am currently interested in are the strength of compactness statements of relational structures in ZF (see Logical compactness and constraint satisfaction problems for an introduction or The CSP Dichotomy, the Axiom of Choice, and Cyclic Polymorphisms for one of the main results already established on that topic) and interactions between topology and algebra, where my PhD supervisor, Michael Pinsker, just obtained an interesting new result (All mixed identities are singular in groups with no algebraicity) in a joint work with our group's former PostDoc Paolo Marimon.
